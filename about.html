About Us

We are your one stop audio & visual solutions outfit

Galaxy Studios is a one stop audio & visual solutions outfit based in Abuja, Nigeria. The establishment is run by prolific musician, sound engineer, producer, & designer Chidubem Kenechukwu Udeoji aka DETAILMADEIT. His qualifications are BSc Management & Business from Skidmore College, and MA Audio Production from University of Westminster. Detail has worked with numerous local and international acts on a multitude of sonic and visual projects. Galaxy Studios not only offers recording & sound production, but it also offers music composition, graphic design and video editing. With a fantastic collection of gear, the latest software's, and over 6 years experience in the music, sound and design industry, GALAXY STUDIOS can offer you creative solutions for any project.
